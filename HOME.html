One of the oldest vintage & classic cars rally in Europe!

Since 1982, Carovana Romantica has been a unique, slow and long distance vintage & classic cars touring rally, open to 45 vintage and classic cars, built from 1918 to 1969.

Welcome to our journey!

Every year we organize a new journey in Italian regions and Mediterranean countries, full of historical, cultural and, why not, "romantic” suggestions, driving 800/900 km in 8 days. Pre-War and prestigious cars have priority in enrollment.
